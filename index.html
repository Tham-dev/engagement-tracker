How to log a touchpoint

  1. Click Log Touchpoint from any page
  2. Fill in Account Name, Date, Sent By, Tier, Channel
  3. Enter the Campaign / Email Name
  4. If account has hit the touch limit in 14 days → set Cooling Off = Yes
  5. Check Dashboard for instant status update
